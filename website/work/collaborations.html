micromanagement, performance art institute, sf, 2011
An evening of performative artworks curated by Sean Fletcher and Isabel Reichert. Sixteen experimental performers spent the evening observing, controlling, attending to minutia, and insisting on results — imposing double binds, questioning competence, and setting up audiences to fail.

ship of fools: limbo, 2010–17
A participatory project exploring the concept of Limbo — a state of neglect, confinement, or oblivion. Referencing Hieronymus Bosch and Sebastian Brant, participants identified aspects of themselves creating stagnation and performed these "fools" on camera, recorded from the waist down.
