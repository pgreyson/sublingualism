secret society (rhythm society), sf, 2007
Video Matic Lantern — a projector mounted on one end and a hemispheric mirror on the other, with the projection corrected to undistort the image onto a cylindrical screen. The screen was made by weaving wax paper together and fusing it with an iron. Suspended above the dance floor on steel cables. This is the only surviving photo.

byob (bring your own beamer), gray area, sf, october 2024
With Hope Cox. The video pipeline is a 3TrinsRGB into a Lo-Fi Future T-420. The Structure applies a chroma or luma based stereo mapping as well as some color adjustments and the result is fed back into the 3TrinsRGB as luma. The Video Matic Lantern is visible in the background.
