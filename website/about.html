Stereoscopic video art from modular synthesis — patterns generated in hardware, processed through custom shaders, output as side-by-side 3D.

The name is a double meaning. Sublingual — work that is sub-linguistic, not intended to be described or labeled. In the Buddhist tradition of direct perception, there is experience before the mind names it. I am a vipassana practitioner and I consider this work to be related to that practice.

The other meaning is a wink. I've shown this work many times at events where various substances are consumed with much the same intent as the meditative process — to bypass normal perception and have direct experience. Absorbed under the tongue.

Any phone-based VR viewer works. A $15 cardboard viewer and a phone is all you need.
